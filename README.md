## Hey, I am Suhana

I am an MSc Cybersecurity candidate at TUS Athlone, Ireland, specialising in **EU AI Act compliance** and **GDPR**. I build open-source tools that make AI governance practical for companies that do not have a team of lawyers.

My thesis built an automated NLP pipeline that detects GDPR compliance gaps in EHR privacy policies using SBERT and Legal-BERT. My current project is a suite of five open-source EU AI Act compliance tools, built because no free, accurate, interactive versions of these existed.

I am an **IAPP member** currently working toward **CIPP/E** certification and looking for roles in AI governance, data protection, and compliance in Ireland from September 2026.
